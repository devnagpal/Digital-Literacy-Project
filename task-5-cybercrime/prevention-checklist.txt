Prevention Checklist: Stay Safe Online
• Use strong, unique passwords for every account.
• Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible.
• Never share OTPs, UPI PINs, or passwords with anyone.
• Do not click unknown links in SMS, email, or WhatsApp.
• Verify UPI requests before approving any payment.
• Check the sender’s handle and app name before trusting a message.
• Use only official banking and payment apps.
• Do not install remote-access apps for “help” or “verification.”
• Keep phone and apps updated regularly.
• Report cyber fraud quickly through the National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in.
• In case of financial fraud, call 1930 immediately.
• Inform your bank or payment provider as soon as possible.
